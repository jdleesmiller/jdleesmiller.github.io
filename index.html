About

John has a PhD in Engineering Mathematics from the University of Bristol and a BSc Hons in Computing Science from the University of Alberta. His areas of interest include optimisation, data mining and transportation. His recent work was on dynamic and stochastic vehicle routing for a new urban transportation mode called Personal Rapid Transit (PRT), which means autonomous computer-driven taxis on dedicated roads. He was part of the team that delivered the world's first PRT system at London's Heathrow Airport in 2011 (www.ultraprt.com). He is now a postdoc at the University of Southampton, where he works on statistical methods for anonymously tracking cars through road networks using roadside Bluetooth detectors. He also runs an online scientific writing application called writeLaTeX.
